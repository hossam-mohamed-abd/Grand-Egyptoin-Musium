# Donor / Supporter

## Role
A patron or sponsor interested in financially supporting the museum's mission.

## Goals
- Understand donation options and impact.
- Find clear calls-to-action to contribute.
- Stay informed about events or initiatives they are funding.

## Typical Journey
1. Arrives via homepage or a campaign link and navigates to the Donate page.
2. Reads about donation opportunities and benefits.
3. Checks event details to see programs aligned with their interests.
4. Follows provided instructions or contact details to complete a contribution.

## Features Interacted With
- Donate page content and calls-to-action.
- Events section to see upcoming initiatives.
- Collections highlights that showcase preservation impact.

## Pain Points, Needs, and Expectations
- Requires transparency on how funds are used and any perks for donors.
- Expects secure and trustworthy pathways to contribute (currently informational only).
- Wants responsive contact or follow-up information if online payments are unavailable.

## Permissions and Limitations
- Read-only within the site; depends on external payment or contact flows.
- Cannot manage campaigns or content within the system.

## How the System Helps
- Surfaces donation messaging prominently on the homepage and dedicated Donate page.
- Connects funding motives to events and collections to demonstrate value.
- Provides clear navigation to reach donation information without friction.
